File Naming

How solutions are named in your repository

Format: CP/Codeforces/{rating}/{problemId}_{problemName}.{ext}
Example: CP/Codeforces/800/1234A_Watermelon.cpp

Solutions are organized by their difficulty rating in the repository. Special characters are sanitized automatically.
